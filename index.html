LMC Remote — what's new

The whole room, not just the PC.

Everything that shipped in the LMC build on top of the Media Remote foundation.

📺

TCL Google TV over ADB

Full d-pad navigation, power, volume, mute, HDMI source switching, and a Gemini assistant button — all over ADB Wireless Debugging. Plus built-in quick-launch for Netflix, Prime, Hulu, and YouTube, and 4 customizable slots for any other Android app by package name.

🔍

mDNS auto-discovery

Finds the TV's live IP address and ADB port automatically over mDNS — even after it changes, which happens every time the TV reconnects to Wi-Fi. No more reading the IP off the TV settings screen and pasting it in manually.

🔊

Sonos control

Volume slider and mute for any Sonos speaker on the network. Includes a multi-speaker picker that discovers every available speaker — so you're always controlling the right room, not guessing which one it grabbed.

💡

Shelly Wall Dimmer

Brightness slider, quick preset levels (Off / 10% / 20% / 50%), and full integration into scene automation — Movie Night can auto-dim or shut off the light, Easy Listening sets it to a fixed level, all from one tap.

🎬

Movie Night & Easy Listening

One-tap scene buttons at the top of the Room tab. Each scene has independent toggles for Sonos mute and TV audio — so you can pause just the speakers, just the TV, or both. No complex automation config required.

📱

Installable PWA

Add to your phone's home screen as a real Progressive Web App with a proper app icon — not a generic browser bookmark badge. Works on Android and iOS. The icon and app name show up exactly as you'd expect from a native app.

Setup

Three steps. No wizard.

One exe, one batch file. No background installer, no dependencies, nothing phoning home.

1

Download & unzip

Drop the folder anywhere on the media PC — MediaRemote.exe and Setup.bat just need to stay together.

2

Run Setup.bat

One UAC prompt adds the firewall rule, registers it to start with Windows, and drops a shortcut on your Desktop with the app icon.

3

Open on your phone

The tray icon shows your PC's address on hover. Open it in any browser — or install it as a PWA for the full home-screen experience.
